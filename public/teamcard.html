Head of Youth Journal

Hi, my name is Mahsa. I am in my fifth year of honours Life Sciences at McMaster University. I've always been passionate about research and science communication. My research focus involves finding the mechanism underlying Idiopathic Pulmonary Fibrosis (IPF). I am a firm believer that science thrives through communication. Effective science communication builds support for science, promotes understanding of its wider relevance to society, and encourages more informed decision-making at all levels. As Tellura's Head of Youth Journal, I want to help with making science literature more accessible for younger students.
