about us

Tellura is a student organization, based at McMaster University. Its mission is to provide a set of accessible guides, materials, and opportunities that help high school and middle school students learn about scientific research. By providing such help to younger students, the members of this organization will gain knowledge and experience related to the field of science communication.

You can learn more about our organization here.

If you have any questions, concerns, or comments, please feel free to email tellurascience@gmail.com.
